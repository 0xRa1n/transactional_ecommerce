Product Description


The AMD Ryzen 9 7950X is a high-performance desktop processor designed for enthusiasts and professionals. It features 16 cores and 32 threads, making it ideal for multitasking, content creation, and gaming. With a base clock speed of 4.5 GHz and a boost clock of up to 5.7 GHz, it delivers exceptional performance for demanding applications. The processor is built on AMD's advanced Zen 4 architecture, providing improved efficiency and performance per watt. It supports PCIe 5.0 and DDR5 memory, ensuring compatibility with the latest technologies. The Ryzen 9 7950X is unlocked for overclocking, allowing users to push their systems to the limit. Whether you're a gamer, streamer, or content creator, the AMD Ryzen 9 7950X offers the power and performance needed to tackle any task with ease.

Product Specifications
