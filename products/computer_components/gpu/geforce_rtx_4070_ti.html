Product Description


The MSI GeForce RTX 4070 Ti Gaming X Slim BLACK is a high-performance graphics card designed for gamers and content creators. It features NVIDIA's latest Ada Lovelace architecture, delivering exceptional performance and ray tracing capabilities. With 12GB of GDDR6X memory and a boost clock speed of up to 2610 MHz, this GPU ensures smooth gameplay and fast rendering times. The Twin Frozr 3 thermal design keeps the card cool and quiet during intense gaming sessions. Equipped with multiple display outputs, including HDMI 2.1a and DisplayPort 1.4a, it supports high-resolution monitors and immersive gaming experiences. Whether you're gaming at 1440p or diving into creative work, the MSI GeForce RTX 4070 Ti Gaming X Slim BLACK is built to handle it all.
