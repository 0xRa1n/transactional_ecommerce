Product Description


TP-Link Tapo C200 Pan/Tilt Home WiFi Camera is a versatile security camera that offers pan and tilt functionality for comprehensive home surveillance. It features high-definition video quality, night vision, motion detection, and two-way audio, allowing you to monitor and communicate remotely via a smartphone app. The camera supports easy installation and integrates seamlessly with smart home systems for enhanced security and convenience.

Product Specifications


Video Resolution: 1080p Full HD

Field of View: 360° Pan, 114° Tilt

Night Vision: Up to 30 feet

Connectivity: Wi-Fi 2.4GHz

Audio: Two-way audio with built-in microphone and speaker

Motion Detection: Yes, with customizable activity zones

Storage: Supports microSD card up to 128GB

Compatibility: Works with Tapo app, Amazon Alexa, and Google Assistant
