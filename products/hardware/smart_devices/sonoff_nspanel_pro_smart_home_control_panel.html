Product Description


The SONOFF NSPanel Pro Smart Home Control Panel is an advanced touchscreen device designed to centralize and simplify the control of various smart home appliances and systems. Featuring a sleek design and an intuitive user interface, the NSPanel Pro allows users to easily manage lighting, climate control, security systems, and more from a single location. It supports voice control through popular virtual assistants and offers seamless integration with a wide range of smart home products. With customizable scenes and schedules, the NSPanel Pro enhances convenience and efficiency in managing your smart home environment.

Product Specifications


Display: 7-inch Touchscreen

Resolution: 1024 x 600 pixels

Connectivity: Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n

Compatibility: Works with SONOFF and other smart home devices

Voice Control: Supports Amazon Alexa and Google Assistant

Power Supply: DC 5V/2A

Dimensions: 180mm x 120mm x 15mm

Weight: 300g
