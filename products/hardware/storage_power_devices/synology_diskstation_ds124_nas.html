Product Description


The Synology DiskStation DS124 is a high-performance Network Attached Storage (NAS) device designed for home and small office use. It features a powerful dual-core processor and 1GB of DDR4 RAM, ensuring smooth performance for file sharing, data backup, and multimedia streaming. With support for up to 12TB of storage capacity, the DS124 provides ample space for your growing data needs. It also offers advanced data protection features, including RAID support and automatic backups. The DS124 runs on Synology's intuitive DiskStation Manager (DSM) operating system, providing a user-friendly interface and a wide range of applications for enhanced productivity and entertainment.
